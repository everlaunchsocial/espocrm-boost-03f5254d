## Main Product: AI Voice Employee

EverLaunch AI provides businesses with an AI-powered virtual employee that:

### Core Capabilities
- **AI Phone Answering**: Natural voice conversations that handle inbound calls 24/7/365
- **Website Chat Widget**: Text-based AI chat embedded on customer websites
- **Website Voice Widget**: Voice AI widget for hands-free website interaction
- **Appointment Booking**: Automatically schedules appointments to business calendars
- **Lead Capture & CRM**: Captures contact information and stores leads
- **Call Recordings & Transcripts**: Full recordings and AI-generated transcripts of all calls
- **Lead Notifications**: Instant SMS/email alerts for new leads
- **After-Hours Handling**: Configurable behavior (voicemail, AI answering, etc.)
- **Business Hours Awareness**: AI knows when the business is open/closed
- **Call Transfer**: Can transfer calls to real humans when needed

### Technology Stack
- **Voice Provider**: VAPI (primary), ElevenLabs (alternative)
- **AI Models**: OpenAI GPT, Cartesia voices
- **Phone Numbers**: Dedicated phone numbers provisioned per customer
- **Vertical-Specific Prompting**: AI trained for specific industries

## Affiliate/Partner Program

EverLaunch AI uses a multi-level affiliate program where partners earn commissions for referring customers.

### Affiliate Plans

| Plan | Monthly Fee | Demo Credits/Month | Best For |
|------|-------------|-------------------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | 5 | Casual referrers |
| **Basic** | $29 | 20 | Part-time affiliates |
| **Pro** | $99 | 50 | Active sellers |
| **Agency** | $299 | Unlimited | Sales teams/agencies |

### Commission Structure (3-Tier MLM)

| Level | Commission Rate | Description |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Tier 1** | 30% | Direct sales (your referrals) |
| **Tier 2** | 15% | Referrals from your referrals |
| **Tier 3** | 5% | Third-level referrals |
| **Total** | 50% | Combined across all tiers |

### Commission Math Example
- Customer pays $799 setup → Tier 1 affiliate earns $239.70 (30%)
- Customer pays $249/month → Tier 1 affiliate earns $74.70/month (30%)
- Commissions are **recurring** on monthly subscriptions

### What Affiliates Get
- Personal affiliate dashboard
- Demo creation system (AI-generated demos branded to prospects)
- HeyGen AI video generation (personalized sales videos)
- Avatar profile creation (custom AI avatar with their face/voice)
- Sales training library
- Weekly payouts via direct deposit
- Done-for-you marketing materials
- White-label options available

## Demo System

Affiliates can create personalized AI demos for prospects:

### Demo Creation Process
1. Affiliate enters prospect's business name and website URL
2. System scrapes the prospect's website for content
3. AI creates a custom demo trained on the prospect's business
4. Demo includes:
   - Live voice AI widget
   - Live chat widget
   - Custom avatar (if affiliate has one)
   - Branded color scheme
5. Affiliate can send demo link via email with tracking
6. System tracks views, interactions, and conversions

### Demo Features
- Passcode-protected access
- View count tracking
- Chat/voice interaction counts
- Email open tracking
- Conversion tracking
